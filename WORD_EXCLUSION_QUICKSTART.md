# Quick Start: Word Exclusion Filter

## 🎯 In 30 Seconds

1. **Settings** ⚙️ → **Dashboard Settings** tab
2. **Select** your form
3. **Type** words (one per line):
   ```
   thing
   stuff
   ok
   ```
4. **Save** 💾
5. **Reload** dashboard → words disappear from bubble chart ✨

---

## 📍 Where to Find It

```
Sentibyahe App
    ↓
⚙️ Settings (top navigation)
    ↓
📊 Dashboard Settings (3rd tab)
    ↓
Select Form → Enter Words → Save
```

---

## 💭 What Words to Exclude?

**Good to Exclude (Filler):**
- thing, stuff, ok, very, just, like, kind, sort

**DON'T Exclude (Important):**
- clean, fast, friendly, reliable, helpful, safe

**System Names:**
- land, transport, system, app, platform

---

## 🔄 What Gets Affected?

| What | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 📊 Bubble Chart | ✅ Excluded words hidden |
| 📋 CSV Export | ❌ No change (complete data) |
| 💾 Raw Database | ❌ No change (untouched) |
| 🔍 Other Charts | ❌ No change (only bubbles) |

---

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Can I undo this?**  
A: Yes! Click "Clear All" button anytime.

**Q: Does it affect my data?**  
A: No! Only the visualization changes. Your data stays complete.

**Q: When do changes take effect?**  
A: Immediately on next dashboard reload.

**Q: Can different forms have different exclusions?**  
A: Yes! Each form has its own independent list.
